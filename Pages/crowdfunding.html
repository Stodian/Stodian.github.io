Join Us in Building Our Future, One Brick at a Time!

Our construction project is making strides towards creating a better community, and we need your help to bring it to life. With our "Pay for a Brick" crowdfunding initiative, you have the opportunity to leave a lasting mark on our project and be a part of something meaningful.

For every donation you make, you'll be contributing to the purchase of bricks that will be used in the construction of our building. Each brick represents a tangible piece of progress and symbolizes your support for our vision.

Whether you donate the cost of one brick or several, every contribution counts and brings us closer to our goal. Your generosity will be recognized and appreciated, and your name will forever be associated with the success of our project.

How to Donate

  1. Choose the number of bricks you'd like to pay for:
    • Single Brick: $10
    • Bundle of 5 Bricks: $40
    • Bundle of 10 Bricks: $75
  2. Click on the "Donate Now" button below to proceed to the payment page.
  3. Enter your payment details and complete the donation process.
  4. Once your donation is processed, your name will be added to our virtual "Wall of Supporters" to recognize your contribution.
